Common Questions

Find answers to the most common questions we hear from homeowners and business owners in the Greater Toronto Area.

How long does a roof replacement take in Toronto?
Most residential roof replacements in Toronto take 1-3 days depending on the size of your home, weather conditions, and the type of roofing material. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your free consultation and keep you informed throughout the project.
Do you offer emergency roofing services?
Yes, we offer limited emergency roofing services for urgent situations like storm damage, leaks, and structural issues. We respond within 4 hours and can provide temporary repairs to protect your property. Call (987) 654-3211 for immediate assistance.
How do I know if I need a new roof?
Signs you need a roof replacement include missing or curling shingles, granules in gutters, water stains on ceilings, sagging rooflines, or a roof that's 15-20+ years old. We offer free inspections in Toronto, Vaughan, and Markham to assess your roof's condition.
Are you licensed and insured in Ontario?
Yes, we're fully licensed and insured with $5 million in coverage. We also back every project with a 1-year warranty and 5 years on labor, so you can have confidence in our work.
