Our Story

In Our Blood
Roofing Is

I come from a family of roofers—roofing is in our blood. I've been roofing for more than 30 years myself, learning the trade from my father and uncles who built this business on integrity and craftsmanship. When I founded Lizz Roof in Toronto over 15 years ago, I wanted to carry forward that legacy while bringing modern techniques and materials to every project we take on.

Today, we're proud to serve homeowners and businesses across Toronto, Vaughan, and Markham with the same dedication my family has shown for decades. Whether it's a residential roof replacement, commercial flat roofing, or new construction roofing, we approach every job with the care and precision that's defined our family's work for generations.
