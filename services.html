FAQ
How long does a roof replacement take?
Most residential roof replacements in Toronto take 1-3 days depending on the size of your home, weather conditions, and roofing material. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your free consultation and keep you updated throughout the project.
How do I know if I need a new roof?
Common signs include missing or curling shingles, granules in your gutters, water stains on ceilings, sagging rooflines, or a roof that's 15-20+ years old. We offer free inspections to assess your roof's condition and provide honest recommendations.
Are you licensed and insured in Ontario?
Yes, we're fully licensed and insured with $5 million in coverage. We back every project with a 1-year warranty and 5 years on labor. You can verify our credentials during your free consultation.
